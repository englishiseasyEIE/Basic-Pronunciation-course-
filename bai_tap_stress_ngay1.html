🔴 Chữ IN HOA = âm nhấn   ⚪ Chữ thường = không nhấn
1 BƯỚC 1 — LUYỆN TỪ (Word Level)
🎯 A. Từ 2 âm — nhấn âm 1 (Noun/Adj)
HAP-py /ˈhæp.i/ STU-dent /ˈstjuː.dənt/ TA-ble /ˈteɪ.bəl/ WA-ter /ˈwɔː.tə/ GAR-den /ˈɡɑː.dən/ DOC-tor /ˈdɒk.tə/ MOR-ning /ˈmɔː.nɪŋ/ PRET-ty /ˈprɪt.i/
🎯 B. Từ 2 âm — nhấn âm 2 (Verb)
be-GIN /bɪˈɡɪn/ en-JOY /ɪnˈdʒɔɪ/ ar-RIVE /əˈraɪv/ de-CIDE /dɪˈsaɪd/ for-GET /fəˈɡet/ re-PEAT /rɪˈpiːt/ cre-ATE /kriˈeɪt/ sug-GEST /səˈdʒest/
⚡ C. Từ 3 âm — tìm âm nhấn
BEAU-ti-ful /ˈbjuː.tɪ.fəl/ im-POR-tant /ɪmˈpɔː.tənt/ to-MOR-row /təˈmɒr.əʊ/ un-der-STAND /ʌn.dəˈstænd/ ba-NA-na /bəˈnɑː.nə/ com-PU-ter /kəmˈpjuː.tə/

/ˈprɪt.i ˈɡɑː.dən/ — 🎵 TUM-ta TUM-ta

🧩 B. Chunk với Verb — nhịp khác
be-GIN to en-JOY
/bɪˈɡɪn tə ɪnˈdʒɔɪ/ — 🎵 ta-TUM ta ta-TUM
de-CIDE to ar-RIVE
/dɪˈsaɪd tə əˈraɪv/
for-GET to re-PEAT
/fəˈɡet tə rɪˈpiːt/

/ə ˈbjuː.tɪ.fəl bəˈnɑː.nə/
⚡ C. Noun vs Verb — cùng chữ, stress KHÁC!
📦 Noun chunk🏃 Verb chunk
a RE-cord player ▶re-CORD a song ▶
a nice PRE-sent ▶pre-SENT the project ▶
a work PER-mit ▶per-MIT the entry ▶
3 BƯỚC 3 — LUYỆN CÂU (Sentence Level)
📝 A. Câu đơn giản — Level ⭐
The STU-dent is HAP-py to-DAY.
/ðə ˈstjuː.dənt ɪz ˈhæp.i təˈdeɪ/
The DOC-tor ar-RIVED this MOR-ning.
/ðə ˈdɒk.tə əˈraɪvd ðɪs ˈmɔː.nɪŋ/
I for-GOT to bring the WA-ter.
/aɪ fəˈɡɒt tə brɪŋ ðə ˈwɔː.tə/
📝 B. Câu trung bình — Level ⭐⭐
The TEA-cher de-CI-ded to be-GIN the im-POR-tant LES-son.
/ðə ˈtiː.tʃə dɪˈsaɪ.dɪd tə bɪˈɡɪn ðə ɪmˈpɔː.tənt ˈles.ən/
She en-JOYS the BEAU-ti-ful GAR-den every MOR-ning.
/ʃiː ɪnˈdʒɔɪz ðə ˈbjuː.tɪ.fəl ˈɡɑː.dən ˈev.ri ˈmɔː.nɪŋ/
📝 C. Câu nâng cao — Level ⭐⭐⭐
The im-POR-tant DOC-tor de-CI-ded to pre-SENT the RE-cord to the HAP-py STU-dent to-MOR-row.
/ðə ɪmˈpɔː.tənt ˈdɒk.tə dɪˈsaɪ.dɪd tə prɪˈzent ðə ˈrek.ɔːd tə ðə ˈhæp.i ˈstjuː.dənt təˈmɒr.əʊ/
I sug-GEST we re-PEAT the EX-er-cise and try to un-der-STAND the com-PU-ter.
/aɪ səˈdʒest wiː rɪˈpiːt ðə ˈek.sə.saɪz ənd traɪ tə ʌn.dəˈstænd ðə kəmˈpjuː.tə/
